Any Questions?

How does the whole process work?

Contact us through the provided email or through any of our other provided social media & tell us about your project in greater detail. Once your project gets reviewed by our team we will reach out to you through email.

How do I go about providing the initial materials?

The most common way how you as the client can provide the initial materials (e.g., logos, text content) is a link to a google doc containing the necessary information, you can also provide the materials needed through email, as long as it gets to us it's perfectly fine.

How do revisions work?

You will be given access to a Trello board where all requests, edits, and communication will be handled.

Can you also create websites?

We can certainly accomplish it! Currently, we are using Jetbrains WebStorm as our main application to develop websites.

What software do you use?

We use Affinity Designer, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Visual Studio Code, and Jetbrains Webstorm

How long does it take to deliver designs

Before receiving the initial materials (e.g., logo, text content) we will give you questions to answer in order to get a better understanding of your project and how you want the end result to be. The first drafts will be given within a few business days after receiving the initial materials and answers to the questions we've sent you.
